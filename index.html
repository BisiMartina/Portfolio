Meet me

Hi! I'm Martina and I am an aspiring UX/UI designer.

I began my academic journey by studying communication from a humanistic perspective, and then moved on to study communication in the digital field. During my studies, I discovered the work of user experience and interface designers, which greatly intrigued me. My goal is to design products and services that can make people's lives more simpler and enjoyable, by make their interaction with technology smoother.
